ANGRY BIRDS

Established in 2013

Angry Birds is a Finnish action-based media franchise created by Rovio Entertainment..

The game series focuses on a flock of birds referred to by the same name who try to save their eggs from green-colored pigs. The first main-series video game sequel, Angry Birds 2, was released on 30 July 2015
